Research

My research interests are focused mainly on mathematics applied to the social sciences, but I am game to work on anything that interests me, which is, well, most things. Along with David McCune and Jennifer Wilson, I am a co-author of the 2023 book, Delegate apportionment in the US presidential primaries—a mathematical analysis, which is part of Springer’s Studies in Choice and Welfare series.
