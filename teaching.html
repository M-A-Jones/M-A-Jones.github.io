Teaching

Since 2009, I have taught a summer class (Mathematics of Decisions, Elections, and Games) for high school students as part of the University of Michigan’s Michigan Math and Science Scholars program. I also volunteer teach (for the last 10 years—minus a year on sabbatical) a special topics course during the school year as part of the Ann Arbor Math Olympiad club.
